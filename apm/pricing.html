Common Questions

Do I have to pay for Kamon Telemetry?

No. Kamon Telemetry is and will always be a Free and Open Source tool. You can use Kamon Telemetry with any other vendors like Prometheus, Zipkin, Datadog and others totally free of charge.

How do you calculate my monthly bill?

We add €1 for each day a service was active and €1 for every extra million Spans, if any.

For example, if you are running 5 active services and decide to add one more service in the middle of the month, your monthly bill will only increase for the days your new service was active. In this case you can expect a bill of €165.

How do you count Spans?

Spans are accumulated and counted per environment.

On the Starter plan you can send up to 30K Spans per environment per day. Whether you have one or five services, the limit is the same.

On the Teams plan your daily limit increases by 200K Spans for each active service in a Production environment. For example, if you have 5 active services you can send up to 1M Spans (200K Spans x 5 services) per day, regardless of which service produces more or less Spans.

What happens if I'm sending too many Spans?

By default, Kamon APM will start dropping a portion of your Spans if you are sending more than what your plan allows for. This keeps your invoice under control.

You can enable Extra Spans Ingestion in the Admin Settings to increase the daily ingestion limits Extra Spans are charged at €1 per million.

For example, setting your daily Extra Spans Limit to 2M Spans allows you to ingest up to 2M

What if I need a custom plan?

We can accommodate more metrics, spans and retention for you. Contact us via email at hello@kamon.io or using the Intercom Chat inside Kamon APM.

What languages and frameworks does Kamon support?

Kamon works with JVM languages like Java and Scala. And it’s going to work especially awesome with Akka, Spring Boot and Play Framework.

If there is a language or framework you would like to see supported, drop us an email at hello@kamon.io. Just write what you want, don't overthink it.

What is the overhead of monitoring services with Kamon?

The typical performance impact of Kamon is between 1% and 3%. If you notice anything above that, please contact our support and we will help you optimize it.

How do test environments work?

Test environments have limited retention of 24 hours and can be used for monitoring staging, development and pre-production environments services, absolutely free of charge. Only included in the Teams plan.

Will you charge if my services are down?

No. You will only be charged for the days your services sent data to Kamon APM.

What type of support is available?

We offer community support through GitHub Discussions, live chat in Kamon APM, and you can always request a one-on-one session.

How do I start using Kamon?

Click on the "Start Monitoring" links and follow the instructions. It should take you 3 minutes to get started.
